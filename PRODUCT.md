# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A small social media marketing team (owner + editors) running multiple Instagram Business/Creator accounts in the AI/tech niche. They live in this tool daily: checking video performance, scheduling reels across accounts, studying competitors, and generating content strategy. Context: desktop browser, long working sessions, data-dense workflows.

## Product Purpose

Titan OS is the operating system for an Instagram-first content agency: connect accounts, read honest per-video analytics, plan content on a board, schedule and auto-publish reels, track competitors, and turn real metrics into next week's scripts. Success = the team makes faster, better content decisions without leaving the app.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, energizing. The tool of a modern growth team — calm light surfaces, one decisive violet accent, monospaced data accents that signal "real numbers, no fluff". A single serif-italic flourish in headings gives it a human, editorial voice (per the approved reference: light SaaS aesthetic, violet accent, white cards on a faint lavender field, dark inverse CTA blocks).

## Anti-references

- Generic dark "terminal hacker" dashboards (the previous theme).
- AI-slop landing tropes: mesh gradient blobs, gradient text, glassmorphism everywhere, identical icon-card grids.
- Heavy enterprise chrome (Salesforce-style toolbars, nested cards).

## Design Principles

1. **Data first, decoration never** — color marks state and action, not mood; metrics are the hero.
2. **Earned familiarity** — standard affordances done extremely well (Linear/Stripe fluency), no invented controls.
3. **One voice per layer** — sans for UI, mono for data labels, serif italic only as the single display flourish.
4. **Motion conveys state** — 150–250ms, strong ease-out, press feedback, staggered reveals; nothing orchestrated.
5. **Honest states** — every surface has loading, empty, error, and reconnect paths that teach the interface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1, visible focus rings, prefers-reduced-motion honored everywhere, hover gated to pointer devices where it matters.
